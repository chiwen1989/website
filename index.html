ABOUT US

A Warm welcome to Brezel-Bakery Project, your friendly specialist for European breads, cakes and pastries. We Like to introduce ourserlves with a few words about us and our philosophy. It all starts with passion and love for baking. Our everyone's gain is to produce only the highest consistent quality possible, every single day!

Made with quality ingredients from Europe, such as flour from Germany and France, butter from France and top shelf chocolate from Switzerland and Belgium just to list a few of many Ingredients.

Our extensive knowledge paired with traditional artisan skills gives us almost unlimited possibilities to create only the best product for our loved customers, as they deserve it! Long fermentation and high hydration of our doughs gives an amazing unique flavour, texture and crust. We are also very proud of our German Sourdough which adds a rich and balanced grade of sourness to breads as the German Sourdough.

As we self-set our standards high and strictly keep to the traditional method of baking, we do not use any convenience products in our bakery. Everything is made daily fresh on premises from our dedicated team, a lot of the fresh produce which is needed on a day to day base is brought in from the local markets such as fruits, salat, egg’s and many more.

We are really looking forward to welcome you in our bakery, to treat yourself with a few sweet goods and enjoy the taste of Europe.
